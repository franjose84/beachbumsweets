About

BeachBum Sweets has been in existence since 2011.  After taking some time off to take care of family we are back with an incredible amount of excitement and passion.  Our goal is to always make sure every person loves eating our treats as much as we loved making them!

We believe in giving back to our community and the world for everything we've been able to do.  It's our passion to help with good causes as often as we can.
